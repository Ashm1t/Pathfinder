Brief Facts:
The complainant states that he supplied textile goods worth Rs. 9,60,000 to
Manoj Tiwari, proprietor of Tiwari Garments, on credit between January and
March 2026. Manoj Tiwari was also entrusted with Rs. 3,00,000 in advance
payment collected on the complainant's behalf from a downstream buyer, which
he was to deposit into the complainant's account but instead used for his
own business expenses. When the complainant demanded settlement, Manoj
Tiwari issued a cheque for Rs. 9,60,000 dated 15.05.2026 which was
dishonoured on presentation due to insufficient funds. He has since stopped
responding to calls.

Sections applied: Section 316 BNS (criminal breach of trust), Section 138
of the Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881 (dishonour of cheque).

Investigating Officer: Sub-Inspector Naveen Kumar, PS Sadar Bazar, Panipat.

Case Status: Under investigation. Accused traced and interrogated.
Chargesheet under preparation.
